Scrambled Eggs with Smoked Salmon

Smoked salmon and scrambled eggs

Scrambled eggs with smoked salmon mixed in, and a sprinkling of herbs on top. A classic.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • smoked salmon
  • milk or cream
  • butter
  • pepper
  • herbs (eg parsley)

Steps

  1. Mix eggs with a little milk and pepper
  2. Melt butter in a pan then add the egg mixture
  3. Stir to craete the 'scrambled' egg texture
  4. Once almost cooked through, add the smoked salmon and stir through to warm
  5. Serve and sprinkle the herbs on top
