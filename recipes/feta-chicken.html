Feta Stuffed Chicken

Chicken breast stuffed with feta and herbs

A cacophony of lemon, parsley, basil, chilli, and delicious feta cheese, stuffed inside a moist chicken breast. Delightful!

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts
  • 100g feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp fresh herbs (parsley, basil, chilli, garlic)
  • lemon zest
  • olive oil
  • 100ml white wine
  • juice of half a lemon

Steps

  1. Butterfly chicken breasts
  2. Mix herbs, lemon zest and feta together
  3. Stuff into chicken breasts. Secure with cocktail sticks.
  4. Season in a pan so each side is golden (3-4mins)
  5. Finish in the oven (20-30mins depending on thickness)
